Pulse Status Guide

Priority flows top to bottom. Product takes the status of the first condition met.

Status Meaning Logic Threshold Action
● Inactive No Stock / Offline Stock = 0 Archive or check feed
● Quality Alert High Return Rate Returns > 5% Check quality & sizing
● Margin Bleed Heavy Discounting Price < 35% of Orig Reduce discount depth
● Broken Sizes Size Fragmentation Avail < 50% Restock missing sizes
● Restock ASAP Running Out Supply < 4 Weeks Reorder immediately
● Low Conversion High Views, No Sales View > 1k & ATC < 1% Review price & image
● Overstock Moving Slowly Supply > 20 Weeks Run promo to clear
● Hidden Gem High Potential View < 200 & ATC > 4% Increase marketing
● Healthy Performing Well No issues found Maintain strategy
